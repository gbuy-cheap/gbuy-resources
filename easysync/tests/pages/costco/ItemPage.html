						The Iridium power bank features a powerful 10,000mAh battery in a compact size for safe, reliable power. 

    Charge most mobile phones, tablets or other micro USB devices at full speed with two powerful 2.4A USB outputs. A sleek, powerful and stylish solution for all of your charging needs. 

    • 10,000mAh battery charges most phones 3-4 times
    • Safe, reliable power with advanced power management for all devices 
    • Dual 2.4A USB outputs charges devices at high speed 
    • Industry-leading 4.8A/24-watt total output 
    • 2A micro USB input 
    • 5A/25W magnetic charge connector for attaching power bank to the included dock. Re-charges the Iridium power bank in around two hours- Up to 500% faster than standard power banks. 
    • LED Charge indicators
    • Compact size measures 5.2” L x 2.7” W x .7” D
    • Durable soft touch finish with stainless steel accent 

    High Speed Charging Station Features:

    The Iridium high speed charging station features an incredible 61 watts of power that provides high speed charging for the Iridium power bank and up to three additional devices. Compact and sleek design fits well in any environment. 

    • 5A/5V (25 watt) magnetic connector charges the iridium power bank in around 2 hours- up to 500% faster than standard charging methods.
    • Three additional 2.4A (7.2A total) USB outputs charge three additional devices at full speed. 
    • LED Charge indicator
    • Compact size measures 3” W x 5.1” T x 4” D. 
    • Durable soft touch finish with stainless steel accent 

    Micro USB Cable: 
    • Charge-up in style with the included 3’ premium woven micro USB cable. 
    • Versatile 3’ length
    • Durable and tangle-free mesh jacket 
    • Fray proof rubber and aluminum connectors 
    • Supports 2.4A charge speeds for rapid charging 
    • Grey and silver color looks great alongside all devices 

    What’s Included: 
    -	10,000mAh (3.8V/38Wh) lithium ion power bank
    -	High Speed Charging Dock with 5A/5V (25W) magnetic connector and three 2.4A/5V (36W) USB Outputs 
    -	3’ PET Mesh Micro USB Cable with aluminum connectors 
    -	AC power cord 
    -	Instruction manual
