    Caring for shoes since 1906.KIWI Foam Polish Applicator helps apply paste or cream shoe polish quickly and easily.
    • Caring for shoes since 1906.KIWI Foam Polish Applicators help apply paste or cream shoe polish quickly and easily. The built-in rim on the reusable applicator helps to keep fingers clean.

    Warnings:

    Warning Text: KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN AND PETS.

    Directions:

    Instructions: 1. Remove any dirt from shoes. 2. Apply shoe polish with Kiwi Polish Applicator. 3. Allow polish to dry, 4. Brush with Kiwi Shine Brush. 5. Buff to a shine with Kiwi Shine Cloth. To best prolong the life of your shoes, polish every 5 to 10 wearing occasions.
